eQrepository Platform

eQrepository is a B2G platform that enables public transport authorities to efficiently create and manage transit networks and schedules in GTFS format, ensuring real-time accuracy and ease of maintenance.

Transit Network

Set up the transit network by adding terminals, stops, routes, and depots manually or by importing stop data with latitude/longitude coordinates. Existing GTFS Static data files can also be imported.

Schedules

Create GTFS-based schedules to stay updated. Manage routes, stops, departure, and arrival times to match passenger demand and traffic conditions.

GTFS Static

Easily generate GTFS-based schedules to stay updated. Manage routes, stops, departure, and arrival times to match passenger demand and traffic conditions
